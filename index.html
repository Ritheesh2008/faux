Optimistic Braking™

Engage brakes and hope! Stopping distance measured in geological time. Features "Are We There Yet?" technology that automatically adjusts expectations to match deceleration rate.

"All-Weather" Tires

Made from recycled rubber bands. Equally ineffective in all conditions. Special "hydroplaning mode" activates automatically on wet leaves or painted lines.

👀

Large body panel gaps offer unobstructed views of the road beneath you. Optional "see-through" package available for doors that may detach while driving.

Vehicle so light it may right itself in wind. Cardboard Edition features "automatic flat pack" mode for easy storage after accidents.
